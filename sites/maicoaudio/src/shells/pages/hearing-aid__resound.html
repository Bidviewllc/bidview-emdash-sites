ReSound Hearing Aids

Maico Audiological Services is an authorized provider of ReSound hearing aids in Newport News, VA, Chesapeake, VA, and Smithfield, VA.

ReSound hearing aids are designed to help you hear clearly in both quiet and noisy environments, while also offering modern features like Bluetooth® streaming, rechargeable batteries, and tinnitus support. With options to fit mild to profound hearing loss, ReSound hearing aids make it easier to stay connected, confident, and in control of your hearing every day.

ReSound Hearing Aids in Virginia

ReSound Enzo IA

The ReSound Enzo IA is designed for severe to profound hearing loss. It is the world’s smallest rechargeable Super Power hearing aid and the first of its kind to support Bluetooth LE Audio and Auracast broadcast streaming.

With a stylish, durable design, the ReSound Enzo IA is built for the most challenging listening situations. It offers exceptional clarity, feedback control, and all-day battery life. 

Unique Perks and Features:

  • Clear Focus: Automatically reduces background noise while highlighting speech.
  • 360 All-Around listening: Naturally shifts attention between voices and surrounding sounds.
  • Comfortable and weatherproof: Lightweight, all-day wear with protective nanocoating.
  • Flexible connectivity: Supports Bluetooth® LE Audio, Auracast™, and direct streaming from multiple devices.

ReSound Vivia

The ReSound Vivia delivers clear, natural sound in a variety of listening environments. Built on ReSound’s latest platform, it uses advanced sound processing and artificial intelligence (AI) to intelligently adjust to your surroundings. It also has All Access Directionality feature to help you focus on speech while still being aware of sounds around you. The Ultra Focus mode gives you an extra boost when listening to someone directly in front of you in challenging situations. Hear conversations with ease in noisy places, enjoy music, or follow dialogue on TV without needing to turn up the volume in uncomfortably loud levels.

Key Features:

  • Long-lasting battery (even while streaming)
  • Durable, weather-resistant
  • Bluetooth and Auracast for smooth device and public audio connections
  • Auracast Assistant for hearing announcements in public venues

ReSound Savi

ReSound Savi brings excellent sound quality, comfort, and the latest wireless features at an accessible price point. It boasts of all-day power for streaming and listening and also comes with Bluetooth® & Auracast™ technology.

Key Features:

  • Water and dust resistance
  • Auracast Assistant for public audio access
  • Hands-free calling with a simple tap

ReSound Nexia

The ReSound Nexia combines exceptional sound clarity with powerful, high-tech connectivity. It is the first hearing aid line to support Bluetooth LE Audio and Auracast broadcast audio. Stream audio directly in public spaces like theaters, airports, or places of worship with no extra receiver needed. This is recommended for people with mild to profound hearing loss.

Key Features:

  • Advanced Bluetooth® for efficient streaming and long battery life
  • Auracast™ to hear public broadcasts directly through your hearing aids
  • Smart 3D App for remote support, tinnitus management, and sound adjustments
  • CROS system compatibility for single-sided hearing loss

ReSound Omnia RIE & Mini RIE

ReSound Omnia is ideal for mild to severe hearing loss. Available in Receiver-in-Ear (RIE) and Mini RIE designs, these devices are designed for speech clarity, all-day comfort, and natural sound.

Key Features:

  • Bluetooth for hands-free calls and media streaming (iOS & Android)
  • “Check My Fit” tool for correct device placement
  • Remote app access for adjustments and tinnitus support
  • ReSound Relief App for calming tinnitus therapy

ReSound Omnia ITE

ReSound Omnia RIE hearing aids is backed by ReSound’s exclusive Organic Hearing system, combining directional listening with an ambient sound experience. The Mini RIE offers all the Omnia benefits in the smallest RIE format. This device is recommended if you are looking for a smart and powerful hearing aid that offers discretion and ease of wear.

Key Features:

  • Wireless Bluetooth streaming
  • App-based controls and remote adjustments
  • “Check My Fit” for proper use
  • Built-in tinnitus relief tools

ReSound Omnia BTE & Power BTE

The ReSound Omnia BTE (Behind-the-Ear) and Omnia Power BTE offer a reliable hearing solution without compromising design, comfort, and sound quality. Whether you have moderate, severe, or profound hearing loss, the Omnia BTE family offers a dependable, durable option that fits busy and active lifestyles.

Key Features:

  • Bluetooth® streaming for calls and media
  • “Check My Fit” tool for optimal placement
  • Smart 3D App for personalized adjustments
  • Tinnitus therapy options for added relief

Frequently Asked Questions – ReSound Hearing Aids

What are the different technology levels in ReSound hearing aids?

ReSound hearing aids usually come in three technology tiers to suit a variety of needs:

  • Level 9: most advanced features for active lifestyles and complex listening environments
  • Level 7: a balanced mix of premium and core features
  • Level 5: essential functionality for everyday hearing support

Our audiologist and hearing aid specialist in Chesapeake, VA, Smithfield, VA, and Newport News, VA can help you decide which level is best for your hearing and lifestyle.

How can I adjust my ReSound hearing aids?

You can adjust your ReSound hearing aids in a few easy ways. Many models allow you to control volume and programs directly on the device using small buttons. You can also use the ReSound Smart 3D™ app on your smartphone to make quick changes, such as adjusting volume, switching listening programs, or fine-tuning sound for noisy environments. Some models even let your hearingcare provider make remote adjustments through the app, so you can get support without visiting the office.

Contact us to learn more about available hearing aids in Smithfield, VA.

How long do ReSound hearing aids last?

With proper care, most ReSound hearing aids last about 5 to 7 years. Their lifespan depends on how often you wear them, how well you maintain them, and the environments you’re in each day. Regular cleaning and professional checkups help extend their life. Many people choose to upgrade sooner to take advantage of new technology, but with good care, your ReSound hearing aids can provide many years of reliable support.
