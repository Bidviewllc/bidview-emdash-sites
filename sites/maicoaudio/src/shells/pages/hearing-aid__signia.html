Frequently Asked Questions About Signia Hearing Aids

What is the top-of-the-line Signia hearing aid?

The Signia Pure Charge&Go IX, built on the newest Integrated Xperience (IX) platform, is currently the most advanced model. Its RealTime Conversation Enhancement helps follow multiple speakers in real time, making it ideal for busy or noisy places.

What are the levels of Signia Pure Charge&Go technology?

The Pure Charge&Go IX comes in five technology levels: 1 IX, 2 IX, 3 IX, 5 IX, and 7 IX. All provide great sound support, but higher levels include stronger noise reduction, more focus on speech, and advanced streaming options.

Can I adjust my Signia hearing aid?

Yes. With the Signia app, you can easily adjust volume, switch programs, and even fine-tune sound balance between bass and treble, all right from your smartphone.
