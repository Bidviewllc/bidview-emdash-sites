FAQs

Which hearing aids are the most advanced?

The best hearing aid depends on your needs, but many newer models include Bluetooth, rechargeable batteries, and noise reduction.

Which brands help with tinnitus?

Oticon, Widex, and Starkey have special sound therapy programs.

How long do custom ear molds last?

Custom ear molds typically last 2 to 5 years. Their lifespan depends on how well you care for them and whether your ears change shape over time.

How do I clean my custom earplugs or ear molds?

Wipe them each day with a soft, dry cloth. For a deeper cleaning, you can use mild soap and warm water. Make sure they are completely dry before wearing them again. Avoid using harsh chemicals, as these can damage the material and shorten their lifespan.
