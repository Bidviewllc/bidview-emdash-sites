How do I know if I need a hearing aid?

If youâ€™re finding it hard to follow conversations (especially in noisy places) frequently increasing the TV volume, or feeling left out during social gatherings, it may be time for a hearing evaluation. An audiologist at Maico Audiological Services can assess your hearing and determine if a hearing aid is the right solution for you.

What is the difference between hearing aids and personal sound amplification products (PSAPs)?

Hearing aids are FDA-regulated medical devices designed to treat hearing loss. They are professionally fitted and programmed to your hearing needs. PSAPs, however, are general amplifiers meant for individuals without hearing loss and do not provide the same level of customization, support, or effectiveness.

Can I get a hearing aid without a hearing test?

Yes, over-the-counter (OTC) hearing aids are available without a hearing test and are designed for adults with mild to moderate hearing loss. However, having a professional hearing evaluation at Maico Audiological Services ensures your hearing aid is matched to your specific needs and helps rule out any underlying medical conditions.

How long do hearing aids last?

Most hearing aids last between 3 to 7 years, depending on the model, how well theyâ€™re maintained, and how often theyâ€™re used. Regular cleanings, proper storage, and routine check-ups with your audiologist or hearing instrument specialist can help extend the life of your devices. As technology improves, many people choose to upgrade sooner to benefit from the latest features and better performance.
