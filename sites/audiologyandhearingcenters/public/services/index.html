What we Do

Our Audiology Services

If you’re experiencing hearing difficulties, tinnitus, or other hearing related issues, our team is here to help.

Hearing Evaluations

A comprehensive hearing evaluation helps identify hearing loss and determine the best solutions for your needs. Our team uses advanced testing to assess your hearing and recommend personalized treatment options.

View Service

Tinnitus Evaluations and Support

Tinnitus, or ringing in the ears, can be frustrating and disruptive. Our tinnitus evaluations help pinpoint the cause, and our treatment options, including sound therapy and management techniques, can help you find relief.
View Service

Diagnostic Services

We offer a range of diagnostic services to assess hearing health and detect any underlying conditions. Our team ensures accurate results, guiding you toward the best treatment plan.
View Service

Hearing Aid Services

From choosing the right hearing aid to ongoing support, we provide expert care to help you get the most out of your device. Our team ensures proper fitting, adjustments, repairs, and long-term maintenance.
View Service

Hearing Aid Fittings

A properly fitted hearing aid makes all the difference. We customize your device for comfort and optimal performance, ensuring a natural listening experience tailored to your needs.
View Service

Hearing Aid Repair

If your hearing aid isn’t working as it should, we offer fast and reliable repairs to restore its functionality. Whether it’s a simple fix or a more complex issue, we can help.
View Service

Enhance your hearing

Hearing Aids & Protection

Hearing loss shouldn’t keep you from enjoying life—or worry about how hearing aids might look. The truth is, when you hear better, you engage more, and most people won’t even notice your hearing aids.

At Audiology & Hearing Centers, we provide discreet, affordable hearing solutions tailored to your needs. Our services include:

  • Budget-friendly hearing aids
  • Complete hearing care and support
  • Custom-fit options for maximum comfort
  • A satisfaction guarantee for peace of mind
  • Ongoing care to keep you hearing your best

Take the first step toward better hearing today!
