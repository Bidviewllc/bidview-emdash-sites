ReSound Hearing Aid Models Available In Granger, IN

ReSound ReSound Enzo IA™

The ReSound Enzo IA is designed for severe to profound hearing loss. It is the world’s smallest rechargeable Super Power hearing aid and the first of its kind to support Bluetooth LE Audio and Auracast broadcast streaming.

With a stylish, durable design, the ReSound Enzo IA is built for the most challenging listening situations. It offers exceptional clarity, feedback control, and all-day battery life. 

Unique Perks and Features:

  • Clear Focus: Automatically reduces background noise while highlighting speech.
  • 360 All-Around listening: Naturally shifts attention between voices and surrounding sounds.
  • Comfortable and weatherproof: Lightweight, all-day wear with protective nanocoating.
  • Flexible connectivity: Supports Bluetooth® LE Audio, Auracast™, and direct streaming from multiple devices.

ReSound Vivia™

ReSound Vivia™ is a smart hearing aid built to deliver natural, clear sound wherever you are. It uses brain-friendly technology to help you focus on what matters-whether you’re in a quiet room or a noisy setting.

Unique Features:

  • All-Day Battery Life – Keeps up with calls, music, and your day
  • Weather-Resistant Design – Protection from water and dust
  • Bluetooth & Auracast™ – Connects easily to phones, TVs, and public broadcasts
  • Auracast Assistant – Helps you hear announcements in places like theaters and airports

ReSound Savi™

ReSound Savi™ offers comfort, ease of use, and reliable performance. With a sleek design and smart connectivity, it helps you stay connected with clear, natural sound.

Unique Features:

  • Long-Lasting Battery – All-day power, even with streaming
  • Water and Dust Resistant – Built to last
  • Bluetooth & Auracast™ – Stream from your favorite devices
  • Auracast Assistant – Hear public audio announcements clearly
  • Hands-Free Calling – Answer calls with a simple tap

ReSound Nexia™

ReSound Nexia delivers clear, lifelike sound through its Organic Hearing™ system. It lets you stream calls, music, and video from iPhones, iPads, and select Android devices. You can even use it as a headset for Mac computers.

A CROS system is also available for people with hearing loss in one ear-it sends sound from the weaker ear to the stronger one.

Unique Features:

  • Advanced Bluetooth Streaming – Enhanced audio quality and battery efficiency
  • Auracast Broadcast Audio – Hear public broadcasts directly in your hearing aids
  • App & Remote Help – Adjust settings and manage tinnitus through the ReSound Smart 3D app

ReSound Omnia RIE & Mini RIE

ReSound Omnia provides natural sound and all-day comfort. Choose between Receiver-in-Ear (RIE) or Mini RIE styles-both great for mild to severe hearing loss. Rechargeable and battery-powered versions are available.

Unique Features:

  • Bluetooth Connectivity – Seamlessly connects to smartphones and tablets
  • Fitting Assistance – The Check My Fit tool helps ensure proper placement
  • App & Remote Help – Fine-tune your hearing aids from your phone
  • Tinnitus Relief – The ReSound Relief app offers sound therapy and relaxation exercises

Frequently Asked Questions About ReSound Hearing Aids

What levels of technology are available with ReSound hearing aids?

ReSound Omnia hearing aids come in three levels:

  • 9 (Premium) – Full-featured technology
  • 7 (Advanced) – Great balance of features and value
  • 5 (Basic) – Essential features for everyday hearing

How do I adjust my ReSound hearing aids?

You can use the ReSound Smart 3D app on your iPhone or Android device to adjust volume, settings, and even manage tinnitus.

How long do ReSound hearing aids last?

Most hearing aids last about five years, depending on care and usage. Many users upgrade sooner to take advantage of newer features and technology. Your audiologist can help you decide the right time to upgrade.
