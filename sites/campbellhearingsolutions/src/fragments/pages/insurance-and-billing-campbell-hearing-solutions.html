Insurance and Billing

At Campbell Hearing Solutions, we want to make your care experience as smooth and transparent as possible. Whether you’re using insurance or paying out-of-pocket, we are committed to helping you understand your rights, your protections, and your options when it comes to medical billing.

Insurance Information

We accept a variety of insurance plans. 

Before your visit, we recommend checking with your insurance provider to confirm coverage for hearing evaluations, hearing aids, and other services.

If you’re not sure whether your insurance covers our services, our team is happy to help guide you through the process. 

Please call our office and we’ll work with you to verify your benefits and answer your questions.

Have Questions?

Campbell Hearing Solutions is here to answer your questions about billing, estimates, or insurance coverage. 

Please don’t hesitate to contact our friendly staff and audiologists in Granger, IN!
