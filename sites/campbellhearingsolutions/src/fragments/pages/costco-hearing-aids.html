Costco Hearing Aids

Is Costco the Right Place for Hearing Aids?

If cost is your biggest concern, Costco might seem like a good place to start. But it’s important to understand the trade-offs.

Most Costco hearing centers are staffed by hearing aid dispensers, not audiologists. While they can assist with basic fittings, they may not be trained to diagnose or manage more complex hearing concerns, such as tinnitus, sudden hearing loss, or inner ear conditions.
