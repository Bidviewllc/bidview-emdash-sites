Frequently Asked Questions About Signia Hearing Aids

What is the most advanced Signia hearing aid?

Signia’s latest technology is found in their AX platform, including Styletto AX, Pure AX, and Insio Charge&Go AX. These models use Augmented Focus™ to separate speech from noise, making conversations clearer in every setting.

What are the technology levels of Signia Pure Charge&Go AX?

Signia offers five technology levels: 1AX, 2AX, 3AX, 5AX, and 7AX. The higher the level, the more advanced features and sound customization you get.

Can I adjust my Signia hearing aids myself?

Yes! The Signia app allows you to change volume, switch programs, and even fine-tune sound quality (like bass and treble) directly from your smartphone.
