Starkey Hearing Aids

Campbell Hearing Solutions is an authorized provider of Starkey hearing aids in Granger, IN.

Starkey is a trusted leader in hearing technology, known for creating advanced hearing aids that help people hear better in all types of situations. Starkey hearing aids are small, custom-fit, and comfortable, making them a reliable choice for a variety of listening environments.

Starkey also uses artificial intelligence (AI) to improve sound quality and make listening easier. Their wide range of hearing aids supports different hearing needs and lifestyles.

Current Starkey Hearing Aid Models

Starkey Omega AI

Starkey Omega AI is built with industry-leading technology that gives users powerful sound clarity and day-long comfort. Its advanced AI system constantly analyzes your surroundings to help you hear speech clearly, stay aware of important sounds, and move through different environments with ease.

 

If you struggle with hearing in noisy places, Omega AI’s Edge Mode+ provides on-demand help by sharpening speech instantly, even in challenging spaces like restaurants, shopping centers, or family gatherings.

 

These hearing aids are also designed for durability. With a 10x more durable waterproof coating, Omega AI can handle rain, sweat, and everyday wear without worry. Rechargeable options give all-day power, keeping you connected from morning to night.

 

Unique Features:

 

  • Edge Mode+: Boosts speech clarity during tough listening moments.
  • Waterproof & Everyday-Proof: Designed for active lifestyles and all-day comfort.
  • Auracast™ & LE Audio Ready: Allows streaming from TVs, smartphones, public announcement systems, and more.
  • Starkey App (Now Powered by Gen AI): Use TeleHear AI for instant, AI-based sound adjustments.

Starkey Signature Series

The Starkey Signature Series gives you amazing sound in hearing aids that are super tiny and almost invisible. They are custom-made to fit inside your ear, so no one can tell you’re wearing them. You’ll hear your friends, family, and the world around you clearly, without anyone knowing why. Whether you want rechargeable, adjustable, or the smallest size possible, there’s a signature style just for you.

 

Unique Features:

 

  • Discreet: Nearly invisible and fits deep in your ear.
  • Natural Sound: Clear, lifelike sound so you can enjoy every moment.
  • Rechargeable: Up to 38 hours of power—no tiny batteries to change.
  • Waterproof: Designed to handle water up to 1 meter deep.
  • Custom Fit: Made just for your ears for all-day comfort and security.

Starkey Edge AI

Starkey Edge AI hearing aids use advanced AI technology to improve sound clarity and reduce background noise. A powerful chip helps you focus on speech, even in noisy places like restaurants or parties.

These hearing aids automatically adjust to your surroundings, offering speech clarity up to 30% better than older models.

Unique Features:

  • Built to Last: Waterproof up to 1 meter and resistant to sweat
  • Strong Bluetooth Connection: Pairs with smartphones and supports Auracast™ for TV and public audio streaming
  • Health & Safety Tools: Includes balance tracking and fall detection
  • Easy App Control: Use the My Starkey app to adjust settings, control volume, stream calls, and locate lost hearing aids (compatible with smartphones and Apple Watch)

Starkey Genesis AI RIC

Starkey Genesis AI hearing aids use intelligent technology to help you hear soft sounds more clearly, understand speech better, and reduce listening effort. These receiver-in-canal (RIC) hearing aids work well for mild to severe hearing loss.

They are available with rechargeable or disposable batteries. The rechargeable version offers up to 51 hours of use on a full charge and provides up to 3 hours of power with just a 7-minute charge.

Unique Features:

  • Waterproof: Protective coating guards against sweat, rain, and debris
  • Bluetooth Connectivity: Use the My Starkey app for settings, streaming, and device location
  • Remote Support: Get help through TeleHear video appointments
  • Tinnitus Relief: Custom sound therapy to ease ringing in the ears

Starkey Genesis AI ITE

The Genesis AI ITE (in-the-ear) hearing aid is custom-made to fit your ear comfortably and securely. Its smart processor mimics the way the brain processes sound, offering natural clarity in any setting.

This model works for mild to severe hearing loss and is available in rechargeable or disposable battery options. It includes Starkey’s smallest wireless CIC (completely-in-canal) model for a nearly invisible fit.

Unique Features:

  • Waterproof: Protective layers shield against water, sweat, and dust
  • Bluetooth Connectivity: Pairs with smartphones for easy control
  • Remote Support: Adjustments available through video support
  • Tinnitus Relief: Personalized sound options to reduce ear ringing

Starkey CROS

Starkey CROS hearing aids are designed for individuals with hearing loss in one ear. They capture sound from the non-hearing side and send it to the better-hearing ear, helping improve conversation and sound awareness.

These hearing aids are compatible with Genesis AI models and select previous Starkey models.

Starkey Evolv AI BTE

Starkey Evolv AI behind-the-ear (BTE) hearing aids adjust automatically to your environment, making it easier to hear clearly in different situations. These hearing aids perform 55 million adjustments per hour to reduce background noise like wind or mechanical sounds.

Suitable for mild to severe hearing loss, they are available in rechargeable or disposable battery options.

Unique Features:

  • Bluetooth Connectivity: Use the Thrive Hearing Control app to adjust settings
  • Remote Support: Receive help through video consultations
  • Tinnitus Relief: Built-in sound programs to help with ear ringing

Starkey Evolv AI Power Plus BTE

The Evolv AI Power Plus BTE offers the same features as the standard Evolv AI BTE but with extra amplification for severe to profound hearing loss. This model uses size 13 disposable batteries for longer-lasting power.

Unique Features:

  • Bluetooth Connectivity: Compatible with the Thrive Hearing Control app
  • Remote Support: Adjustments made through virtual appointments
  • Tinnitus Relief: Custom sound options for tinnitus management

Frequently Asked Questions About Starkey Hearing Aids

What are the tech levels of Starkey Genesis AI?

Starkey Genesis AI hearing aids come in four technology levels:

  • 24 (Premium)
  • 20 (Advanced)
  • 16 (Standard)
  • 12 (Basic)A CROS model is also available for individuals with hearing loss in one ear.

What app does Starkey Genesis AI use?

The My Starkey app pairs with Genesis AI hearing aids. It allows users to adjust settings, track health data, and locate lost hearing aids.

How long do Starkey hearing aids last?

In-the-ear hearing aids typically last around five years, while behind-the-ear models often last five to six years. With proper care, they may last longer. Many people choose to upgrade earlier to benefit from the latest technology. Your audiologist can help determine when it’s time for new hearing aids.
