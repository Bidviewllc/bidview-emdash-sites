Frequently Asked Questions About Unitron Hearing Aids

What is the difference between Unitron Blu and Vivante?

Unitron Vivante (V) is the latest version of receiver-in-the-canal (RIC) hearing aids, replacing Unitron Blu (B). It offers:

  • A new, modern design
  • Improved sound quality
  • A better Remote Plus App

 

For custom in-the-ear (ITE) hearing aids, Unitron Blu remains the most up-to-date option.

What are the levels of technology in Unitron?

Unitron hearing aids come in four levels: 3, 5, 7, and 9.

  • Higher levels (like 9) have better sound processing and stronger noise reduction.
  • They also automatically adjust to noisy environments for clearer hearing.

How long does it take to charge a Unitron hearing aid?

When charging your hearing aids:

  • The green lights blink about 5 times.
  • Once the lights stay solid green, your hearing aids are fully charged.
  • Charging takes about 5 to 7 hours.
