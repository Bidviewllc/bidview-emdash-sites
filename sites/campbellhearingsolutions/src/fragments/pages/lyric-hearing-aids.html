Lyric Hearing Aids

Campbell Hearing Solutions is an authorized provider of Lyric hearing aids in Granger, IN.

Are you looking for hearing loss solutions other than traditional hearing aids? Lyric hearing aids may be a worthy device to check. Lyric comes in a very discreet design and does not need to be removed daily.

What Makes Lyric Different?

Lyric hearing aids are unlike anything else on the market. These ultra-small devices are inserted deep into the ear canal by one of our trained professionals and remain there for weeks at a time. You don’t take them out to sleep, shower, or exercise. They stay in place 24/7, delivering natural, clear sound by using your ear’s natural anatomy to direct sound to your eardrum.

No daily maintenance. No visible parts. Just better hearing with zero hassle.

Why Patients Choose Lyric

Many of our patients in Granger and surrounding communities choose Lyric because they:

  • Want a completely invisible hearing aid
  • Prefer a “set-it-and-forget-it” experience
  • Struggle with changing tiny batteries or managing settings
  • Want the freedom to wear their hearing aid while showering, sleeping, or working out

 

Lyric hearing aids offer a discreet yet effective hearing solution for people diagnosed with mild to moderate hearing loss.

Subscription-Based, Personalized Hearing Care

Lyric is offered on a subscription basis. This means that you don’t simply purchase the device.

A Lyric provider will insert a new Lyric device at regular intervals, ensuring it stays comfortable and effective.

When you avail a Lyric subscription, this includes device fittings, replacements, updates, and ongoing care.

Lyric may be ideal if you:

  • Have mild to moderate hearing loss
  • Have normal-sized ear canals
  • Want a discreet, low-maintenance option
  • Value natural sound quality and comfort

 

However, if you have narrow ear canals, certain skin sensitivities, or more complex hearing needs, we’ll help you explore other options that may be a better fit.

Comfortable, Invisible, Worry-Free Hearing

The biggest advantage of Lyric is how effortless it is. You can wear headphones, go about your daily routine, and even sleep on your side without removing your hearing aid.

It’s a simple solution that makes a big difference in how you feel about managing hearing loss.
