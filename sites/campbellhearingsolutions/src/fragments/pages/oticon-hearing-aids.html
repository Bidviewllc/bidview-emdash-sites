Oticon Hearing Aid Models Available in Granger, IN

Oticon Zeal

Oticon Zeal is the first hearing aid designed to do it all while staying completely unseen. Built for people who want powerful technology in an ultra-discreet design, Zeal delivers exceptional sound clarity, all-day comfort, and seamless connectivity in one tiny device no one will notice.

Zeal transforms the idea of what a hearing aid should be. Its small, nearly invisible design sits comfortably in your ear, giving you the confidence to live fully without drawing attention to your device. Powered by Oticon’s advanced BrainHearing™ technology, Zeal supports the brain’s natural way of processing sound. Enjoy clear, balanced, and comfortable hearing, even in noisy places.

Unique Features:

  • Made to Connect: Stream calls, meetings, music, and more directly from your phone, Apple Watch, TV, computer, or tablet. Zeal brings your digital world to life with smooth, high-quality wireless audio.
  • Reliable Rechargeability: No disposable batteries. With fast, dependable charging, you get all-day power that keeps up with your lifestyle.
  • Same-Day Fitting: Zeal is designed for convenience, offering the option for same-day fitting so you can begin hearing better right away.

Oticon Jet PX

Oticon Jet PX is a smart hearing aid that uses advanced technology (called Deep Neural Network or DNN) to help you hear clearly. With features like Wind Noise Management and Speech Guard, it helps block out sounds that get in the way of talking to others. 

If you have ringing in your ears (tinnitus), Jet PX has built-in sounds to help you feel better. You can also connect it to your phone to listen to music, take calls, or change settings using the Oticon app. It comes in different colors and styles, and you can even get a rechargeable version.

Unique Features:

  • AI-powered OpenSound Navigator with DNN: Improves hearing with clear, natural sound.
  • Tinnitus SoundSupport: Ideal for users with tinnitus as it gives access to customizable relief sounds.
  • SuperShield: Quickly adapts and prevents feedback before it occurs.

Oticon Intent

Oticon Intent is a smart hearing aid that adapts in real-time to your environment. Using 4D Sensor Technology, it tracks your movement, speech, and surroundings to give you the best hearing experience wherever you go.

Powered by Deep Neural Network (DNN) technology, Oticon Intent mimics how the brain naturally processes sound, helping you understand speech more clearly and with less effort.

Key Features:

  • Bluetooth® LE Audio – Connects to multiple devices for direct audio streaming
  • Self-Calibrating Speaker – Adjusts sound automatically for better clarity
  • Fast Charging – Charges 33% faster for all-day use

Oticon Real

Oticon Real is designed for clear everyday listening-even in noisy settings. It reduces sudden distracting sounds like wind or hair movement. With DNN technology trained on 12 million real-life sounds, it helps you understand speech better in real life situations.

Available in miniRITE (receiver-in-the-ear) and miniBTE (behind-the-ear) styles, Oticon Real is suitable for mild to profound hearing loss and comes in both battery and rechargeable versions.

Key Features:

  • Bluetooth Streaming – Works with iPhone® and Android™ devices
  • App Control – Adjust settings with the Oticon Companion or Oticon ON app
  • Tinnitus Relief – Includes Tinnitus SoundSupport™ for soothing sound therapy

Oticon Own

Oticon Own uses smart technology to help you hear better in noisy environments like restaurants or family gatherings. It separates speech from background noise to make conversations easier to follow.

This hearing aid is custom-molded to fit your ears perfectly. Your hearing professional will take an ear mold to ensure a secure and comfortable fit.

Key Features:

  • Bluetooth Streaming – Connects to iPhone® and Android™
  • App Control – Manage settings through the Oticon apps
  • Tinnitus Relief – Includes Tinnitus SoundSupport™ for relief from ringing

Oticon Zircon

Oticon Zircon offers clear sound with balanced volume so voices don’t sound muffled or overly loud. It also provides 360-degree hearing, so you can hear sound from all around without turning your head.

Zircon includes Bluetooth for connecting to your phone, TV, and more. A rechargeable version is also available, offering convenience and all-day use.

Key Features:

  • Bluetooth Streaming – Direct connection to smart devices
  • App Control – Manage your hearing aid through the Oticon apps
  • Tinnitus Relief – Features soothing sounds through Tinnitus SoundSupport™

Oticon CROS

Oticon CROS helps individuals with single-sided deafness by transmitting sound from the weaker ear to the stronger one. It supports both CROS (no hearing in one ear) and BiCROS (some hearing in the better ear).

Oticon CROS works with many models, including:

  • Oticon Real™
  • Oticon More™
  • Oticon Zircon
  • Oticon Opn S™
  • Oticon Play PX
  • Oticon Xceed

 

Key Features:

  • TwinLink™ Bluetooth – Stream calls and music while hearing from both sides
  • App Control – Make adjustments with Oticon’s apps
  • Tinnitus Relief – Includes sound therapy for tinnitus management

Oticon Play PX

Oticon Play PX is designed for children and supports speech and language development. It is the first pediatric hearing aid with DNN technology, helping kids hear better at school and during play.

Available in miniRITE and miniBTE styles, it supports slight to severe hearing loss and comes in battery or rechargeable options.

Key Features:

  • Tamper-Resistant – Safe battery compartment for kids
  • Water-Resistant (IP68) – Protects against dust and moisture
  • LED Indicator – Lets caregivers monitor device status
  • Bluetooth Streaming – Works with smartphones and tablets

 

Additional Accessories for Children:

  • EduMic – Classroom microphone to improve teacher’s voice clarity
  • Amigo FM – Helps kids hear over distance and noise
  • ConnectClip – Streams voices and sounds from up to 65 feet away

Oticon Xceed

Oticon Xceed is built for those with severe to profound hearing loss. It delivers loud, clear, and natural sound, even in noisy environments. Xceed offers 360-degree hearing, reducing background noise so you can focus on what matters most.

Using BrainHearing™ technology, it helps your brain process sound more naturally, reducing listening fatigue. It also offers Bluetooth connectivity and a rechargeable option.

Key Features:

  • Bluetooth Streaming – Connects directly to devices
  • App Control – Easily manage settings with Oticon apps
  • Tinnitus Relief – Soothing sounds for tinnitus comfort

Frequently Asked Questions About Oticon Hearing Aids

How long do Oticon hearing aids last?

Most in-the-ear hearing aids last around 5 years, and behind-the-ear styles can last 5-6 years. With proper care, they can last even longer, but many people upgrade to enjoy new technology.

What is Oticon’s newest hearing aid?

As of March 2026, Oticon’s newest and most innovative hearing aid is the Oticon Zeal.

While the Oticon Intent™ (flagship behind-the-ear model) received a significant hardware expansion in late 2025, the Zeal is the newest standalone product, officially hitting the U.S. and global markets in January 2026.

Which Oticon hearing aids help with tinnitus?

Many Oticon hearing aids include Tinnitus SoundSupport™, which offers soothing sounds to reduce tinnitus symptoms. These models include:

  • Oticon Intent
  • Oticon Real
  • Oticon Own (except CIC/IIC models)
  • Oticon Zircon
  • Oticon CROS
  • Oticon Xceed
  • Oticon Play PX (for children with tinnitus)
