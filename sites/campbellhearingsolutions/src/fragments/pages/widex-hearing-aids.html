Widex Hearing Aids

Campbell Hearing Solutions is an authorized provider of Widex hearing aids in Granger, IN.

Widex is a leading brand in hearing technology, known for bringing artificial intelligence (AI) into hearing aids. Widex hearing aids offer a unique blend of advanced AI features and human-centered design to deliver clear, natural sound.

They’re also an excellent choice for people with tinnitus (ringing in the ears), providing sound therapy to help reduce discomfort and improve daily life.

Current Widex Hearing Aid Models

Widex Allure

The Widex Allure carries the brand’s most advanced hearing aid platform, offering the perfect blend of cutting-edge technology and elegant design. It is crafted to deliver the most natural hearing experience possible, helping you reconnect with the world through clear speech, accurate environmental sound processing, and intuitive adjustments.

Unique Perks and Features:

  • Powered by the W1 Chip: Widex’s fastest chip delivers quick, clear, and natural sound.
  • Allure PureSound™ with ZeroDelay Technology: Helps you hear speech without distortion or echo, creating a more lifelike experience.
  • Speech Enhancer Pro: Lowers background noise so voices stand out, making conversations easier—even in noisy places.
  • Dynamic Feedback Controller: Adapts to your ear’s shape to reduce annoying whistling or squealing sounds.
  • Smooth Streaming: Enjoy music, podcasts, and calls with steady sound—no sudden volume changes or outside interference.
  • Widex Allure App: Easily adjust settings and personalize your hearing experience right from your smartphone.

Widex SmartRIC

The Widex SmartRIC is designed to improve hearing in noisy environments. Its L-shaped design sits higher on the ear, helping make speech clearer. The battery lasts up to 37 hours on a full charge, giving you reliable power throughout the day.

The compact, portable charger holds a week’s worth of power, so you can stay connected wherever you go. It also includes an enhanced microphone cover that reduces background noise for clearer conversations.

Key Features:

  • App: Works with the Widex Moment app to customize your hearing
  • Bluetooth: Connects to both iPhone and Android devices for easy streaming
  • Tinnitus Relief: Includes Zen sound therapy to help manage tinnitus

Widex Moment Sheer RIC

The Widex Moment Sheer RIC (receiver-in-the-canal) combines a sleek, modern design with an upgraded charger. Using dual AI technology, it delivers a personalized sound experience.

Ideal for mild to severe hearing loss, this model is available in both rechargeable and disposable battery versions. The battery lasts around 29 hours without streaming or 16 hours with 8 hours of streaming.

Key Features:

  • Bluetooth: Works with iPhone and Android devices
  • App: Adjust settings through the Moment app
  • Tinnitus Relief: Zen programs provide calming sounds for tinnitus relief

Widex Moment RIC

The Widex Moment RIC is a compact hearing aid that delivers natural sound and faster sound processing, making it easier to hear clearly-especially in noisy places. Many users report higher satisfaction with this model compared to older hearing aids.

This option supports mild to severe hearing loss and comes in rechargeable or disposable battery styles.

Key Features:

  • Bluetooth: Stream audio from iPhone and Android devices
  • App: Customize your hearing settings easily
  • Tinnitus Relief: Includes Zen sound therapy for tinnitus management

Widex Moment BTE

The Widex Moment BTE (behind-the-ear) uses AI technology to learn your sound preferences. The SoundSense Learn feature adapts to your listening environment for more personalized hearing.

This model works for mild to severe hearing loss and is available in rechargeable or disposable battery options (size 13).

Key Features:

  • Bluetooth: Pairs with iPhones and Android devices
  • App: Make easy adjustments using the Moment app
  • Tinnitus Relief: Soothing Zen sounds to reduce ringing in the ears

Widex Moment CROS

The Widex Moment CROS is specially made for individuals who can hear in only one ear. It transfers sound from the non-hearing ear to the hearing ear, improving sound awareness and speech clarity in noisy settings.

This system supports both CROS (one good ear) and BiCROS (hearing loss in both ears, but worse in one) configurations. It also uses less power than many other CROS devices, making it more efficient.

Widex Moment ITE

The Widex Moment ITE (in-the-ear) hearing aid is custom-made to fit your ear comfortably. It adjusts automatically to give you a clear, natural sound experience.

Perfect for mild to moderate hearing loss, it comes with disposable size 10 or 312 batteries, offering up to 105-150 hours of use depending on the model and listening habits.

Key Features:

  • Custom Fit: Personalized design for a secure and comfortable fit
  • Connectivity: Works with Bluetooth and Widex remote control apps
  • Tinnitus Relief: Includes Zen programs to ease tinnitus symptoms

Widex Assistive Listening Devices

Widex also offers additional tools to enhance your hearing experience:

  • Widex TV Play – Streams TV audio directly to your hearing aids
  • Widex Sound Assist – Functions as a microphone, hands-free tool, and remote control
  • Com-Dex, RC-Dex, TV-Dex – Extra accessories for improved hearing aid control

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best Widex hearing aid?

The Widex Moment Sheer 440 is the top-tier model. It offers the most advanced features, including enhanced speech clarity and wind noise reduction.

What are the levels of Widex Moment technology?

Widex Moment hearing aids are available in three levels:

  • Widex Moment 220 – Basic level
  • Widex Moment 330 – Standard level
  • Widex Moment 440 – Premium level

Is Widex Moment good for tinnitus?

Yes! Widex Moment hearing aids come with Zen sound therapy programs to help reduce the impact of tinnitus. These relaxing sounds make the ringing in your ears less noticeable and easier to manage.
