Stacey Linville

Practice Manager

Stacey has been with our team for nearly a decade. She has a bachelor’s degree in organizational management as well as impressive experience in customer service, HR, payroll, and onboarding. Before joining Campbell Hearing Solutions, Stacey spent many years working as a Clinical Coordinator in a large Orthopedic clinic; she also has over 20 years of supervisory experience working in a hospital laboratory.

Stacey has worked in the medical field since she was in high school. She finds this field to be very rewarding as she loves to coach and mentor staff, as well as help patients improve their health.

Outside of work, Stacey enjoys spending time with her family and friends, crafting, fishing at her lake house, and watching her grandkids’ sports.
