ReSound Hearing Aids

Table of Contents
  • ReSound hearing aids are designed to support natural listening and clear speech in the real-world environments you experience every day.
  • Advanced connectivity, personalization tools, and tinnitus support help reduce listening effort and improve comfort over time.
  • Raleigh Hearing and Tinnitus Center is an authorized provider of ReSound hearing aids in Raleigh, NC.

ReSound is known for hearing technology that fits naturally into everyday life, from work conversations and family gatherings to quiet moments at home. At Raleigh Hearing and Tinnitus Center, we recommend ReSound hearing aids for patients who want a comfortable, connected listening experience with technology that adapts smoothly as environments change.

Our approach includes thoughtful counseling, real-world testing such as speech-in-noise evaluation, and precise verification so your hearing aids support daily communication, not just test results.

Why Choose ReSound Hearing Aids

ReSound technology is built around a simple goal: helping hearing feel more natural and manageable in the places you actually live. Many ReSound hearing aids emphasize:

  • Clearer speech with reduced listening effort
  • Seamless connectivity for calls, media, and devices
  • Tools that support personalization and long-term comfort

For many patients, the combination of the right technology and a careful fitting process makes a meaningful difference, especially when understanding speech in noise is a concern.

ReSound Hearing Aid Models We Offer

ReSound Enzo IA

ReSound Enzo IA is designed for individuals with severe to profound hearing loss. It offers powerful amplification and a rechargeable Super Power option built for challenging listening environments.

Key features include:

  • Clear Focus to reduce background noise and highlight speech
  • 360-degree listening for balanced sound awareness
  • Durable, weather-resistant design
  • Support for Bluetooth LE Audio and Auracast broadcast streaming

ReSound Nexia

ReSound Nexia is built on ReSound’s Organic Hearing approach and supports strong connectivity for hands-free calls and streaming from compatible devices. Nexia also offers a CROS solution for single-sided hearing loss.

Key features include:

  • Bluetooth LE Audio for efficient streaming
  • Auracast broadcast audio support
  • ReSound Smart 3D app with remote fine-tuning options
  • ReSound Assist for virtual support
  • CROS option for unilateral hearing loss

ReSound Vivia

ReSound Vivia is designed to make listening feel easier across both quiet and busy environments by supporting focus on what matters most.

Key features include:

  • All-day power, including during streaming
  • Protective coating for water and dust resistance
  • Bluetooth and Auracast support
  • App-based tools for quick access to public broadcasts

ReSound Savi

ReSound Savi offers a streamlined, comfortable listening experience with modern connectivity that fits naturally into daily routines.

Key features include:

  • Reliable all-day battery performance
  • Weather-resistant coating
  • Bluetooth and Auracast support
  • Hands-free calling compatibility with select devices

ReSound Omnia

ReSound Omnia is designed to balance natural sound, comfort, and everyday connectivity. It is available in multiple styles to match a wide range of hearing needs and preferences.

ReSound Omnia RIE and miniRIE

Receiver-in-the-ear options for mild to severe hearing loss, available with rechargeable or disposable battery choices.

ReSound Omnia Custom ITE (ITC, CIC, IIC)

Custom in-the-ear options designed for a personalized fit with reduced interference from glasses and natural wind shielding.

ReSound Omnia BTE and Power BTE

Behind-the-ear and power options for broader ranges of hearing loss, including more significant needs. Omnia is recognized for supporting speech understanding in noise through its beamforming approach.

Omnia highlights include:

  • Natural sound and comfortable fit
  • Strong streaming and connectivity features
  • App-based support and remote care tools
  • ReSound Relief tinnitus support features

ReSound Apps and Support

ReSound offers app-based features that support comfort, confidence, and ongoing personalization, including:

  • Quick adjustments and custom listening programs
  • Remote fine-tuning options, when available

We help patients understand which tools are most helpful for their daily life and how to use them comfortably without feeling overwhelmed.

Assistive Listening Devices and ReSound Accessories

ReSound accessories can provide added clarity in situations where hearing is more challenging, such as distance listening, group conversations, or television viewing.

Common options include:

  • TV Streamer
  • Phone Clip
  • Multi Mic
  • Remote Control

Accessories are recommended based on your listening goals and how you prefer to interact with technology.

Ongoing Service and Support

Hearing aid care continues long after you choose a device. At Raleigh Hearing and Tinnitus Center, we provide cleanings, maintenance, fine-tuning, manufacturer repair coordination, and follow-up visits based on your real-world listening experiences.

If you already own ReSound hearing aids, even if they were purchased elsewhere, we are happy to evaluate and support them. We may also service select older ReSound models.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long do ReSound hearing aids last?

With regular care and maintenance, ReSound hearing aids typically last three to five years, depending on usage and environment.

Can ReSound hearing aids connect to my phone?
Many ReSound models offer direct smartphone connectivity for calls and streaming. Compatibility depends on your phone and the specific ReSound model.
Do ReSound hearing aids help with tinnitus?
Many ReSound hearing aids support tinnitus comfort through sound options and app-based tools, which can be combined with counseling and aural rehabilitation strategies.
