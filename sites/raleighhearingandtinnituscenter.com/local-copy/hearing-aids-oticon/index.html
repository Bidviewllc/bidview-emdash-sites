Oticon Hearing Aids

Table of Contents
  • Oticon hearing aids are built on research focused on how the brain processes sound, supporting more natural and balanced listening.
  • Advanced Oticon technology helps reduce listening effort, improve speech clarity, and support comfort in noisy or dynamic environments.
  • Raleigh Hearing and Tinnitus Center is an authorized provider of Oticon hearing aids in Raleigh, NC.

Oticon is one of the most trusted names in hearing technology, known for decades of research centered on how the brain understands sound. At Raleigh Hearing and Tinnitus Center, we recommend Oticon hearing aids for patients who want hearing that feels natural, comfortable, and supportive of everyday communication.

Each Oticon device is carefully selected, professionally fit, and verified to align with your hearing needs, lifestyle, and long-term goals.

Why Choose Oticon Hearing Aids

Oticon’s approach goes beyond simply making sounds louder. Their BrainHearing™ philosophy is designed to give your brain better access to sound, helping conversations feel clearer while reducing listening effort throughout the day.

Oticon hearing aids are often a good fit for individuals who:

  • Feel mentally fatigued after listening
  • Struggle to follow conversations in background noise
  • Want hearing that sounds more natural and balanced
  • Value research-driven, evidence-based technology

Oticon Hearing Aid Models We Offer

We offer and support a wide range of Oticon hearing aid models, allowing us to match technology to your hearing needs and preferences.

Oticon Zeal

Oticon Zeal is designed for individuals who want advanced hearing technology in an ultra-discreet, invisible style. Built on Oticon’s BrainHearing philosophy, Zeal supports natural sound processing while remaining completely unseen in the ear.

Key features include:

  • Invisible-in-the-ear design options
  • BrainHearing technology for reduced listening effort
  • Wireless connectivity for compatible devices
  • Rechargeable options available

Oticon Intent

Oticon Intent uses advanced sensor technology to adapt to your listening environment in real time. By responding to movement, conversation cues, and surroundings, Intent supports clearer speech with less effort.

Key features include:

  • Real-time sound adaptation
  • Bluetooth LE Audio for high-quality streaming
  • Fast charging with dependable all-day power

Oticon Jet PX

Oticon Jet PX brings Oticon’s modern sound processing and Deep Neural Network technology to a flexible and accessible hearing solution. It is designed to improve speech clarity while managing background noise in everyday listening environments.

Key features include:

  • Deep Neural Network sound processing
  • Speech Guard technology for clearer speech
  • Wind Noise Management
  • Bluetooth connectivity
  • Tinnitus SoundSupport available

Oticon Real

Oticon Real is designed to reduce disruptive sounds such as wind or handling noise while preserving speech clarity, helping everyday listening feel more comfortable.

Key features include:

  • Environmental noise and wind reduction
  • Bluetooth streaming for compatible devices
  • Tinnitus SoundSupport options available

Oticon Own and Oticon Own SI

Oticon Own and Own SI are custom, in-the-ear hearing aids created for individuals who prefer a discreet solution. These devices are custom molded for comfort while delivering clear, natural sound.

Key features include:

  • Invisible-in-Canal (IIC) and Completely-in-Canal (CIC) options
  • BrainHearing technology in a custom-fit design
  • Durable, moisture-resistant construction

Oticon Zircon

Oticon Zircon provides 360-degree sound awareness, helping users stay engaged with their surroundings while reducing listening effort.

Key features include:

  • Direct Bluetooth streaming
  • App-based control and adjustments
  • Tinnitus SoundSupport available

Oticon Xceed

Oticon Xceed is designed for individuals with severe to profound hearing loss, providing powerful amplification while maintaining clarity and comfort.

Key features include:

  • Strong, stable amplification
  • Bluetooth streaming and app control
  • Tinnitus SoundSupport available

Oticon CROS

Oticon CROS is designed for individuals with single-sided hearing loss. It wirelessly transmits sound from the non-hearing ear to the better-hearing ear to improve awareness and balance.

Key features include:

  • Wireless sound transmission
  • App-based control
  • Compatibility with multiple Oticon platforms

Oticon Accessories and Assistive Technology

Oticon offers a range of accessories that support hearing in challenging situations, including phone calls, television listening, and group conversations. Options may include remote microphones, TV adapters, and wireless connectivity tools designed to improve clarity and convenience.

Ongoing Support for Oticon Hearing Aids

Our care does not end after fitting. We provide continued adjustments, cleanings, maintenance, and repair services for both current and older Oticon models. Even if your Oticon hearing aids were purchased elsewhere, we are happy to evaluate and support them.

Please contact us to learn more about Oticon hearing aids we repair and service.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long do Oticon hearing aids last?

With proper care and regular follow-up, Oticon hearing aids typically last three to five years.

Do Oticon hearing aids help with tinnitus?
Many Oticon models include Tinnitus SoundSupport, which may help reduce tinnitus awareness and improve listening comfort.
Which Oticon model is right for me?
The best Oticon model depends on your hearing needs, listening environments, and personal preferences. Your audiologist will guide you through options based on what matters most to you.
