Widex Hearing Aids

Table of Contents
  • Widex hearing aids are designed to deliver exceptionally natural, lifelike sound with minimal delay or distortion.
  • Advanced sound processing and tinnitus support help reduce listening effort and improve comfort in everyday environments.
  • Raleigh Hearing and Tinnitus Center is an authorized provider of Widex hearing aids in Raleigh, NC.

Widex is widely recognized for hearing technology that prioritizes sound quality, comfort, and how the brain experiences sound. At Raleigh Hearing and Tinnitus Center, we recommend Widex hearing aids for patients who want hearing that feels natural and balanced, especially in dynamic or noisy environments.

Each Widex device is carefully selected, professionally fit, and verified to support your hearing needs, listening environments, and personal preferences, with thoughtful follow-up care to support long-term success.

Why Choose Widex Hearing Aids

Widex technology focuses on reducing sound delay and distortion so speech and environmental sounds feel clear and natural. Their platforms are designed to support how the brain processes sound, helping reduce listening fatigue and improve comfort throughout the day.

Widex hearing aids are often a good fit for individuals who:

  • Are sensitive to sound quality or distortion
  • Struggle with speech clarity in background noise
  • Experience listening fatigue
  • Want advanced tinnitus management options
  • Prefer greater personalization and user control

Widex Hearing Aid Models We Offer

We offer and support a full range of Widex hearing aid models, allowing us to match technology to your hearing needs and lifestyle.

Widex Allure

Widex Allure represents Widex’s most advanced platform, combining refined design with cutting-edge sound processing. It is designed to deliver clear speech, accurate environmental awareness, and intuitive adjustments for a more natural listening experience.

Key features include:

  • Fast, natural sound processing
  • Zero-delay sound delivery for improved comfort
  • Enhanced speech clarity in noise
  • Reduced feedback and whistling
  • Smooth Bluetooth streaming and app-based control

Widex SmartRIC

Widex SmartRIC features a unique design that improves microphone placement to support speech understanding in noisy environments. With extended battery life and a portable charger, SmartRIC supports consistent hearing throughout the day.

Key features include:

  • Extended battery life for all-day listening
  • Portable charging for convenience
  • Improved microphone placement for clarity
  • Bluetooth streaming for compatible devices
  • Widex Zen tinnitus support programs

Widex Moment Sheer RIC

Widex Moment Sheer RIC combines advanced sound processing with a sleek receiver-in-canal design. Dual artificial intelligence supports greater personalization and real-time adjustments through the Widex app.

Key features include:

  • Zero-delay sound processing for natural sound
  • Rechargeable or disposable battery options
  • Bluetooth connectivity for streaming
  • Widex Zen tinnitus management programs
  • App-based personalization features

Widex Moment RIC

Widex Moment RIC delivers rapid, accurate sound processing in a discreet design, supporting clear speech and everyday listening comfort.

Key features include:

  • Minimal processing delay
  • Bluetooth streaming and app control
  • Rechargeable and disposable battery options
  • Widex Zen tinnitus support

Widex Moment BTE

Widex Moment BTE hearing aids provide powerful amplification and adaptive sound processing for a wide range of hearing losses.

Key features include:

  • Behind-the-ear design
  • Adaptive sound processing
  • Bluetooth connectivity
  • Tinnitus support options available

Widex Moment CROS

Widex Moment CROS is designed for individuals with single-sided hearing loss. Sound is transmitted wirelessly from the non-hearing ear to the better-hearing ear to improve awareness and balance.

Key features include:

  • Wireless sound transmission
  • Compatibility with Widex Moment platforms
  • Clear sound routing
  • CROS and BiCROS options

Widex Moment ITE

Widex Moment ITE hearing aids are custom-molded for a secure, comfortable fit while providing natural sound and simple operation.

Key features include:

  • Custom in-the-ear design
  • App control via TONELINK
  • Widex Zen tinnitus programs
  • Disposable battery options

Widex Accessories and Assistive Technology

Widex offers accessories designed to support hearing in challenging situations such as phone calls, television listening, and group conversations. 

Options may include: 

  • Widex TV Play
  • Widex Sound Assist 
  • Widex COM-DEX (Legacy / Limited Compatibility)
  • Widex RC-DEX (Legacy / Limited Compatibility)
  • Widex TV-DEX (Legacy / Limited Compatibility)
  • Widex Moment App
  • TONELINK™ App

Ongoing Support for Widex Hearing Aids

Our care continues beyond the fitting. We provide adjustments, cleaning, maintenance, and repair services for both current and older Widex models. 

Even if your Widex hearing aids were purchased elsewhere, we are happy to evaluate and support them.

Please contact us to learn more about Oticon hearing aids we repair and service.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are Widex hearing aids good for tinnitus?
Many Widex hearing aids include Zen tinnitus programs that use sound therapy to reduce tinnitus perception and improve comfort.
How long do Widex hearing aids last?

With proper care and regular follow-up, Widex hearing aids typically last three to five years.

Which Widex model is right for me?
The best Widex model depends on your hearing needs, listening environments, and personal preferences. Your audiologist will guide you through available options.
