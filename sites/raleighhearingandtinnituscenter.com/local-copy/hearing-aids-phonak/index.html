Phonak Hearing Aids

Table of Contents
  • Phonak hearing aids are designed to adapt intelligently to changing listening environments, helping speech remain clear throughout the day.
  • Advanced sound processing and strong Bluetooth connectivity support easier communication, reduced listening effort, and everyday convenience.
  • Raleigh Hearing and Tinnitus Center is an authorized provider of Phonak hearing aids in Raleigh, NC.

Phonak is a global leader in hearing technology, known for developing hearing aids that adapt to the way people live, move, and communicate. At Raleigh Hearing and Tinnitus Center, we recommend Phonak hearing aids for patients who want reliable performance across a wide range of environments, seamless connectivity, and technology designed to support comfortable listening all day long.

Every Phonak device is carefully selected, professionally fit, and verified to support your hearing needs, lifestyle, and long-term success.

Why Choose Phonak Hearing Aids

Phonak hearing aids are built around intelligent sound processing that automatically adjusts to your surroundings. Using advanced operating systems and AI-based sound recognition, Phonak technology helps speech stand out while managing background noise—whether you are in quiet conversation, busy restaurants, or outdoor settings.

Phonak hearing aids may be a good fit for individuals who:

  • Struggle with speech understanding in background noise
  • Move frequently between different listening environments
  • Want strong Bluetooth connectivity with multiple devices
  • Prefer rechargeable and durable hearing aid options
  • Need flexible solutions for unilateral or more significant hearing loss

Phonak Hearing Aid Models We Offer

We offer and support a wide range of Phonak hearing aid models, allowing us to match technology to your hearing needs and preferences.

Phonak Infinio Ultra Line

The Phonak Infinio Ultra Line represents the latest advancement in Phonak technology, designed to support clarity, efficiency, and adaptability across a wide range of listening environments. Built on the Infinio platform, the Ultra upgrade enhances sound recognition and long-term listening comfort.

Infinio Ultra R

A flexible, ultra-adaptive model powered by AutoSense OS™ 7.0, designed to automatically adjust to a wide range of environments. Ideal for patients seeking seamless performance in everyday listening.

Infinio Ultra Sphere

Engineered for complex and noisy situations, this model features Spheric Speech Clarity 2.0 and the breakthrough DEEPSONIC™ AI chip, the world’s first dedicated processor for real-time speech separation. This allows clearer understanding of multiple speakers from any direction, even in the most challenging soundscapes.


Key features include:

  • Advanced automatic sound adaptation
  • Reduced listening effort throughout the day
  • Firmware upgrade compatibility for eligible devices

Phonak Audéo Sphere

Phonak Audéo Sphere is engineered for complex and noisy listening environments. Using advanced AI processing, it helps separate speech from background noise coming from all directions, making it especially helpful in social or group settings.

Key features include:

  • Dedicated AI chip for speech-in-noise clarity
  • Advanced real-time sound processing
  • Rechargeable options and wireless connectivity

Phonak Audéo Infinio

Phonak Audéo Infinio is a receiver-in-the-ear hearing aid designed for clear sound, comfort, and dependable performance in everyday listening situations. It adapts automatically as environments change, helping conversations remain easier to follow.

Key features include:

  • Automatic environment detection and adjustment
  • Rechargeable options with full-day power
  • Bluetooth streaming and app-based control

Phonak Virto Infinio (Custom In-the-Ear)

Phonak Virto Infinio hearing aids are custom-made to fit comfortably inside the ear. Each device is molded to your ear anatomy, offering discreet appearance without sacrificing performance.

Key features include:

  • Custom-molded fit for comfort and security
  • Automatic sound processing
  • Durable, moisture-resistant design
  • Multiple technology levels available

Phonak Audéo Lumity

Phonak Audéo Lumity is designed to help speech stand out more clearly using updated AI-based sound processing. It supports natural communication across quiet and noisy environments.

Key features include:

  • Smart speech-focused sound processing
  • Rechargeable options
  • Bluetooth connectivity and app control

Phonak Slim

Phonak Slim offers the same advanced technology as Lumity in a refined, ergonomic design. Its unique shape is designed to sit comfortably behind the ear, including for individuals who wear glasses.

Key features include:

  • Slim, modern design
  • Rechargeable battery system
  • Bluetooth streaming and easy personalization

Phonak Naída Lumity

Phonak Naída Lumity is built for individuals with more significant hearing loss. It delivers powerful, stable amplification while maintaining clarity and listening comfort.

Key features include:

  • High-powered amplification options
  • Rechargeable and disposable battery choices
  • Bluetooth streaming and app-based adjustments

Phonak CROS Systems

Phonak CROS systems are designed for individuals with single-sided hearing loss. Sound from the non-hearing ear is wirelessly transmitted to the better-hearing ear, improving awareness and balance.

Key features include:

  • Seamless integration with compatible Phonak hearing aids
  • Rechargeable CROS options
  • Improved sound awareness and spatial balance

Phonak Accessories and Assistive Technology

Phonak offers a comprehensive range of accessories designed to improve hearing in challenging situations, including phone calls, group conversations, and television listening. Options may include:

  • Roger™ microphones (Roger On™, Roger Select™)
  • TV Connector
  • PartnerMic™
  • Remote controls and educational solutions

Ongoing Support for Phonak Hearing Aids

Hearing aid care continues beyond the initial fitting. At Raleigh Hearing and Tinnitus Center, we provide ongoing programming adjustments, maintenance, cleaning, and repair services for both current and many older Phonak models, including:

  • Phonak Audéo Paradise
  • Phonak Naída Paradise
  • Phonak CROS Paradise
  • Phonak Naída Link Marvel
  • Phonak Sky Marvel
  • Phonak Audéo Marvel
  • Phonak Bolero B
  • Phonak Naída B
  • Phonak Virto B
  • Phonak Audéo B

Even if your Phonak hearing aids were purchased elsewhere, we are happy to evaluate and support them.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are Phonak hearing aids good for active lifestyles?
Many Phonak models are rechargeable, water-resistant, and designed to perform reliably in dynamic environments.
How long do Phonak hearing aids last?

With proper care and regular follow-up, Phonak hearing aids typically last three to five years.

Do Phonak hearing aids work well in noise?
Phonak technology is designed to automatically manage background noise and support speech clarity in a variety of listening situations.
