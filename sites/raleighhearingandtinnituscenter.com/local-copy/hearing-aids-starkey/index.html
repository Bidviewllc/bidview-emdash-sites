Starkey Hearing Aids

Table of Contents
  • Starkey hearing aids use advanced sound processing and artificial intelligence to support clear speech and comfortable listening in changing environments.
  • Built-in wellness, safety, and tinnitus features help support communication, confidence, and everyday life beyond hearing alone.
  • Raleigh Hearing and Tinnitus Center is an authorized provider of Starkey hearing aids in Raleigh, NC.

Starkey is known for blending innovative hearing technology with features that support daily life, health, and safety. At Raleigh Hearing and Tinnitus Center, we recommend Starkey hearing aids for patients who want dependable speech clarity, intuitive technology, and options that support both communication and overall well-being.

Each Starkey device is carefully selected, professionally fit, and verified to align with your hearing needs, lifestyle, and comfort, with ongoing support to help you hear your best over time.

Why Choose Starkey Hearing Aids

Starkey hearing aids use artificial intelligence to continuously analyze your surroundings and adjust sound automatically. This allows speech to stand out while background noise is managed more effectively, helping listening feel more natural and less effortful throughout the day.

Starkey technology may be a good fit for individuals who:

  • Struggle in noisy or constantly changing environments
  • Prefer hearing aids that adjust automatically
  • Value connectivity, wellness, and safety features
  • Want durable, rechargeable options
  • Need support for tinnitus management

Starkey Hearing Aid Models We Offer

We offer and support a range of Starkey hearing aid models to meet a wide variety of hearing needs and preferences.

Starkey Omega AI

Starkey Omega AI uses advanced artificial intelligence to improve speech clarity and listening comfort across a wide range of environments. On-demand Edge Mode+ provides added support during especially challenging listening moments.

Key features include:

  • Edge Mode+ for enhanced speech clarity
  • Durable, waterproof design
  • Auracast and LE Audio readiness
  • Rechargeable options for all-day power
  • App-based control for easy adjustments

Starkey Edge AI

Starkey Edge AI hearing aids automatically adapt to your listening environment while supporting communication, safety, and awareness throughout the day.

Key features include:

  • Automatic sound adjustments
  • Bluetooth connectivity with Auracast support
  • Built-in wellness and safety features, including fall detection
  • App-based control and customization

Starkey Genesis AI (RIC & ITE Styles)

Starkey Genesis AI uses Neuro Sound Technology and machine learning to deliver clear, natural sound with reduced listening effort. Available in both receiver-in-canal and in-the-ear styles, Genesis AI offers flexibility for many hearing needs.

Key features include:

  • Neuro Sound Technology for natural sound processing
  • Industry-leading rechargeable battery life
  • Water and sweat resistance
  • Remote care support options
  • Multiflex Tinnitus Technology for personalized relief

Starkey Signature Series

The Starkey Signature Series includes custom-made, nearly invisible hearing aids designed to fit deep within the ear canal, offering discretion without sacrificing sound quality.

Key features include:

  • Custom-fit, discreet designs
  • Natural, lifelike sound quality
  • Rechargeable options with extended battery life
  • Waterproof construction for everyday reliability

Starkey Evolv AI (BTE & Power Options)

Starkey Evolv AI behind-the-ear models provide powerful amplification and automatic sound adjustments for individuals with greater hearing needs, including severe to profound hearing loss.

Key features include:

  • High-powered amplification options
  • Bluetooth streaming and app control
  • Remote care support
  • Multiflex Tinnitus Technology

Starkey CROS

Starkey CROS systems are designed for individuals with single-sided hearing loss. Sound from the non-hearing ear is wirelessly transmitted to the better-hearing ear to improve awareness and balance.

Key features include:

  • CROS and BiCROS fitting options
  • Compatibility with multiple Starkey platforms
  • Automatic sound adjustments

Starkey Accessories and Assistive Technology

Starkey offers accessories designed to support hearing in specific situations such as meetings, group settings, and home listening. 

These may include: 

  • Mini Remote Microphone
  • Remote Mic+
  • Table Microphone
  • TV Streamer
  • Remote Control 2.0

Ongoing Support for Starkey Hearing Aids

Care continues beyond the initial fitting. At Raleigh Hearing and Tinnitus Center, we provide ongoing adjustments, cleaning, maintenance, and repair services for current and many older Starkey models, including:

  • Starkey Picasso
  • Starkey Livio
  • Starkey Muse iQ
  • Starkey Halo iQ
  • Starkey Halo 2
  • Starkey Halo

Even if your Starkey hearing aids were purchased elsewhere, we are happy to evaluate and support them.

Please contact us for more information.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long do Starkey hearing aids last?

With proper care and regular follow-up, Starkey hearing aids typically last three to five years, depending on usage and maintenance.

Do Starkey hearing aids help with tinnitus?
Many Starkey devices include Multiflex Tinnitus Technology, which provides customizable sound options to help reduce tinnitus awareness.
What app do Starkey hearing aids use?
Starkey hearing aids use the My Starkey app, which allows users to adjust settings, stream audio, access wellness features, and locate devices.
