Signia Hearing Aids

Table of Contents
  • Signia hearing aids are designed to support clear conversation in real-life listening situations, including busy and changing environments.
  • Advanced speech-focused processing, rechargeable options, and intuitive app control help reduce listening effort and support all-day comfort.
  • Raleigh Hearing and Tinnitus Center is an authorized provider of Signia hearing aids in Raleigh, NC.

Signia hearing aids are a strong option for individuals who want technology that keeps up with everyday life. Whether that means clearer conversations in noisy places, comfortable all-day wear, or simple streaming and control, Raleigh Hearing and Tinnitus Center takes the time to understand how you listen at home, at work, and in the community.

We recommend Signia models that align with your hearing needs and preferences, supported by careful counseling and verification-driven adjustments so your hearing aids work where it matters most.

Why Choose Signia Hearing Aids

Signia is known for combining modern design with speech-focused sound processing and user-friendly control. Many Signia devices are designed to prioritize conversation clarity while you are moving through real environments, not just sitting in quiet settings.

Signia hearing aids may be a good fit if you:

  • Struggle most in noise, group settings, or conversations on the go
  • Want rechargeable options with simple daily use
  • Value Bluetooth streaming and app-based control
  • Prefer discreet styles, including instant-fit and custom options
  • Want access to remote support features through the Signia app

Signia Hearing Aid Models We Offer

Signia Pure Charge&Go BCT IX

Pure Charge&Go BCT IX is a receiver-in-canal hearing aid designed for reliable connectivity and strong conversation support across a wide range of environments.

Key features include:

  • Universal Bluetooth Classic compatibility
  • Up to 36 hours of battery life on a single charge
  • Integrated IX platform with RealTime Conversation Enhancement
  • Discreet receiver-in-canal design
  • Built-in telecoil for supported public venues

Signia Styletto IX

Styletto IX combines refined styling with advanced speech clarity, making it a popular option for patients who want modern design without sacrificing performance.

Key features include:

  • Rechargeable design with compact, portable charging case
  • Bluetooth streaming for calls and media
  • RealTime Conversation Enhancement for dynamic environments
  • Personalized sound support through Signia processing and app features

Signia Pure Charge&Go IX

Pure Charge&Go IX offers a balance of premium sound processing, comfort, and rechargeability for everyday listening.

Key features include:

  • Rechargeable lithium-ion battery for all-day use
  • Bluetooth streaming and app-based control
  • Speech-focused processing for busy environments
  • Comfortable, discreet design

Signia Silk Charge&Go IX

Silk Charge&Go IX is an ultra-discreet, instant-fit in-the-ear hearing aid designed for patients who want a low-visibility option without waiting for custom molds.

Key features include:

  • Instant-fit design with soft silicone Click Sleeves
  • Discreet in-the-ear style
  • Speech clarity support in noise
  • Rechargeable convenience
  • App-based control on select models

Signia Insio IX (IIC/CIC)

Insio IX models are custom-made to fit your ear canal for maximum discretion and comfort.

Key features include:

  • Custom-fit Invisible-in-Canal (IIC) and Completely-in-Canal (CIC) styles
  • Low-visibility designs
  • App-based control options on select models

Signia AX Platform (Pure Charge&Go T AX and Styletto AX)

Signia’s AX platform uses Augmented Focus processing, separating speech and background noise pathways to improve clarity in complex listening environments.

Key features include:

  • Separate processing of speech and noise for improved contrast
  • Bluetooth streaming and app-based control
  • Remote support through the Signia app and TeleCare features

Signia Insio Charge&Go AX

Insio Charge&Go AX is a custom in-the-ear rechargeable option designed for patients who want discreet hearing aids without disposable batteries.

Key features include:

  • Separate processing of speech and noise for improved contrast
  • Bluetooth streaming and app-based control
  • Remote support through the Signia app and TeleCare features

Signia Accessories and Assistive Technology

Signia offers accessories that provide added support in challenging listening situations, including:

  • StreamLine TV for television streaming
  • StreamLine Mic for remote microphone and streaming support
  • StreamLine Remote for simple, discreet control

These tools can be especially helpful in meetings, group settings, places of worship, and home listening environments.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Signia’s newest platform for conversation clarity?
Signia’s IX platform is designed to support real-world conversation, including speech understanding while moving and interacting in dynamic environments.
Can I control my Signia hearing aids from my phone?
Many Signia models can be adjusted through the Signia app, allowing users to change volume, switch programs, and personalize sound settings.
Are there discreet Signia options that still offer strong performance?
Signia offers discreet styles such as Silk instant-fit options and Insio custom IIC and CIC models that are designed to be low visibility while supporting everyday communication.
