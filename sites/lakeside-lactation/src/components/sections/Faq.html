What does a lactation consultant do?

A lactation consultant provides expert support for breastfeeding families, offering education, troubleshooting, and personalized care plans for parents and babies. They help with latch, pain, milk supply, and advise on pumping and supplementation. An IBCLC stands for International Board Certified Lactation Consultant, and is the gold standard in lactation education and care.
