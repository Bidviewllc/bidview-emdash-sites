How Lauren can help

Every feeding journey is different. Whether you’re preparing for birth, struggling with latch or pain, navigating pumping, or transitioning home from the NICU, we offer compassionate lactation support that meets you where you are. Services are available in-home and virtually, making it easier to get help when you need it most.

Prenatal Lactation Consult 

A personalized session to prepare you for feeding before your baby arrives. We review your history, answer questions, and create a plan that builds confidence for your early breastfeeding journey.
