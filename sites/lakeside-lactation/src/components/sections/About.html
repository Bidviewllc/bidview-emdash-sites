Meet Lauren LiButti, RN, IBCLC

After two decades caring for medically complex newborns and guiding parents through some of their most vulnerable moments, Lauren expanded her practice to focus on personalized, hands-on lactation support.

As a mother of two, Lauren also understands breastfeeding challenges firsthand. Her own experiences navigating feeding difficulties shaped her compassionate, judgment-free approach and deep commitment to helping families feel informed, supported, and confident.

Through her private practice, Lakeside Lactation, Lauren provides individualized, evidence-based lactation care to families throughout Lake County and the North Shore suburbs of Chicago. Every visit is designed to leave families feeling reassured, empowered, and equipped with a clear plan forward.
