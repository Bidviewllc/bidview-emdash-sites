Signia Pure Charge&Go BCT IX

A stylish receiver-in-canal (RIC) with built-in Bluetooth Classic for reliable, hands-free calls and streaming. Designed for active lifestyles with strong performance, long battery life, and clear conversation in a wide variety of environments.

Unique features:

  • Universal Bluetooth Classic pairing and stable streaming
  • Up to 36 hours of battery life per charge
  • Integrated Xperience (IX) platform with RealTime Conversation Enhancement for speech in noise
  • Discreet, compact RIC design
  • Built-in telecoil for looped venues

Styletto IX

Elegant, slim design meets high-performance processing. Styletto IX emphasizes speech clarity (even in noise) and offers AI-driven personalization that adapts sound to you.

Unique features:

  • Rechargeable with a compact case (up to three days of on-the-go power)
  • Bluetooth® streaming for music, calls, and media
  • RealTime Conversation Enhancement on the IX platform

Pure Charge&Go IX

Balanced efficiency, convenience, and advanced tech. Excellent speech handling in noisy spaces plus AI-based personalization.

Unique features:

  • Direct Bluetooth streaming to phones, TVs, and more
  • Rechargeable lithium-ion for dependable all-day use

Silk Charge&Go IX

A virtually invisible, instant-fit in-ear option with soft silicone Click Sleeves-no custom molds required. Quick to fit, easy to use, and fully rechargeable.

Unique features:

  • Instant Fit comfort with Click Sleeves

Signia CROS AX (and CROS X)

For single-sided hearing loss/SSD, CROS transmits sound from the unaidable ear to the better ear so you can follow conversation from any side. Available in multiple styles:

  • CROS Styletto AX – slim RIC, portable charging (up to ~20 hours)
  • CROS Pure Charge&Go AX – RIC with up to ~29 hours wear time
  • CROS Pure 312 – RIC using size 312 batteries (up to ~4 days)
  • CROS Silk X – ready-to-wear ultra-discreet CIC (CROS X availability varies by platform.)

Unique features:

  • Bluetooth streaming for Apple/Android (AX CROS/BiCROS) and HandsFree on select iOS with CallControl
  • Signia app controls for aids and CROS, TeleCare, and My WellBeing activity tracking

FAQs – Signia Hearing Aids

What’s Signia’s top model right now?

Pure Charge&Go IX, built on the IX platform with RealTime Conversation Enhancement that tracks and clarifies multiple moving speakers, excellent for busy, noisy environments.

What technology levels does Pure Charge&Go IX offer?

Pure Charge&Go IX has five tiers: 1 IX, 2 IX, 3 IX, 5 IX, 7 IX. Higher levels provide more sophisticated noise handling, voice focus, streaming options, and app features.

Can I adjust my Signia hearing aids myself?

Yes. The Signia app lets you change programs, adjust volume, and tweak sound balance; Signia Assistant and TeleCare offer guidance and remote support.
