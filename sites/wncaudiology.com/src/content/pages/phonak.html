Phonak Audéo Fit

Phonak Audéo Fit is an advanced hearing aid designed for active lifestyles. It helps you build healthy habits by tracking distance, steps, activity levels, average wearing time, and heart rate through the myPhonak app.

Audéo Fit comes in two performance levels, Premium (P90) and Advanced (P70), ensuring it meets your specific lifestyle needs.

  • Universal Connectivity: Pair with Bluetooth®-enabled smartphones, TVs, and Roger™ devices.

Frequently Asked Questions About Phonak Hearing Aids

Does Phonak offer solutions for severe or single-sided hearing loss?

Yes. Phonak provides powerful hearing aids like Naída Lumity for severe to profound hearing loss, as well as CROS options for single-sided deafness.

Can Phonak hearing aids connect to my phone or TV?

Absolutely. Most Phonak models connect directly to smartphones, TVs, and other Bluetooth devices, so you can stream calls, music, and media with ease.

Are there Phonak hearing aids for tinnitus?

Yes. Many Phonak devices include tinnitus management features. The Phonak Tinnitus Balance program provides customizable sound therapy (such as white noise or ocean sounds) that can be tailored by your audiologist.
