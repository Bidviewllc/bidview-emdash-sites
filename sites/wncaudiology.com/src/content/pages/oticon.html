FAQs – Oticon Hearing Aids

How long do Oticon hearing aids last?

With proper care, Oticon hearing aids typically last 5–7 years. Lifespan depends on daily use, maintenance, environment, and technology updates.

How to connect Oticon hearing aids to an iPhone or Android phone?

Integrating your Oticon hearing aids with a mobile device begins with a quick reboot to activate “pairing mode.” This is achieved by either docking the devices in their charging cradle for a moment before taking them out or by using the physical switches to power them down and then back on.

Linking with an iOS Device (iPhone)

Apple devices utilize a dedicated “MFi” (Made for iPhone) interface located within the system settings rather than the general Bluetooth menu.

  1. Enter the Settings menu on your iPhone and scroll down to the Accessibility category.

  2. Choose Hearing Devices to trigger a search for any active hearing technology in the area.

  3. Reboot your hearing aids just before this step to confirm they are in the discoverable window.

  4. Select your name or model from the list of results to initiate the synchronization.

  5. Confirm the pairing prompts that appear; remember to hit “Pair” twice if you are wearing aids in both the left and right ears.

Linking with an Android Device

For the most stable connection on Android, it is best to utilize the official software provided by the manufacturer.

  1. Install the Oticon Companion app via the Google Play Store.

  2. Ensure Bluetooth is active within your smartphone’s shortcut or settings menu.

  3. Launch the Companion app and select the “Connect Now” or “Pair” button on the interface.

  4. Restart your hearing aids so the application’s scanning tool can identify them.

  5. Complete the guided setup on your screen to enable full remote adjustment and direct wireless streaming.
