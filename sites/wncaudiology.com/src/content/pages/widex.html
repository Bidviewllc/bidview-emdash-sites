FAQs – Widex Hearing Aids

How long do Widex hearing aids last?

Most Widex hearing aids last about 3–5 years, depending on use and care. With regular cleanings and check-ups, they can serve you even longer.

Do Widex hearing aids help with tinnitus?

Yes. Many models include Zen Therapy, which plays calming sounds or soundscapes to make tinnitus less noticeable.

Can I stream calls and music with Widex?

Yes. Most modern Widex hearing aids connect directly to iPhones and Android devices for calls, music, and media. The Widex Moment app also allows you to adjust sound and switch programs.
