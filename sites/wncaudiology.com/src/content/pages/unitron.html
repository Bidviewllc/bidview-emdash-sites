FAQs – Unitron Hearing Aids

What is the Unitron Smile platform?

Smile is Unitron’s latest platform (introduced in 2025) designed for effortless, natural listening. Using AI, it recognizes and adapts to multiple listening situations automatically—so you do less fiddling and hear with more confidence.

What technology levels does Unitron offer?

Four tiers: Level 3 (Essential), 5 (Standard), 7 (Advanced), and 9 (Premium). Higher levels add more sophisticated features for speech-in-noise, automatic adaptation, and personalization.

How long does it take to charge a Unitron rechargeable aid?

Most fully charge in about 3 hours. A quick charge (10–60 minutes) typically delivers several hours of use (model/charger dependent).
