Frequently Asked Questions – ReSound Hearing Aids

What are the different technology levels in ReSound hearing aids?

ReSound typically offers three technology tiers:

  • Level 9 – Most advanced features for active lifestyles and complex listening needs
  • Level 7 – Balanced mix of premium and core features
  • Level 5 – Essential functionality for everyday hearing support

Our Asheville, NC audiologists will help you choose the right level for your needs.

How can I adjust my ReSound hearing aids?

You can manage your devices through the ReSound Smart 3D™ app on your iPhone® or Android™. With the app, you can:

  • Change volume and program settings
  • Manage tinnitus relief sounds
  • Get remote support from your audiologist

We’ll be happy to set this up with you during your fitting.

How long do ReSound hearing aids last?

On average, ReSound hearing aids last about five years, depending on use and care. With professional maintenance and regular cleanings, they can often last even longer.
