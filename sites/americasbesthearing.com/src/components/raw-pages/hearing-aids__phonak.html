Current Phonak Hearing Aid Models

Phonak Infinio Ultra Line

The Phonak Infinio Ultra Line represents the next evolution in hearing technology, delivering elevated clarity, efficiency, and adaptability through advanced AI processing. Released in October 2025, this flagship upgrade enhances the foundational Infinio platform with smarter sound recognition, better battery performance, and simplified maintenance.

For many existing Phonak users, these improvements can be activated through an in-clinic firmware update, allowing current Infinio or Infinio Sphere devices to perform at an entirely new level without requiring new hardware.

Key Features:

  • AutoSense OS™ 7.0 adapts with 24% greater precision and is trained on 18× more sound scenarios, resulting in smarter environmental transitions and more natural listening.
  • Spheric Speech Clarity 2.0 (Ultra Sphere only) enhances speech up to threefold in noisy settings by isolating voices and reducing background interference.
  • Listening effort is reduced by up to 35%, supporting long-term comfort and decreasing auditory fatigue.

Phonak Audéo Sphere

A high-tech hearing aid that adapts to different settings, from quiet rooms to noisy cafés.

Key Features:

  • Rechargeable battery for all-day use
  • Smart processing that makes speech stand out
  • Bluetooth for calls, music, and app control

Phonak Audéo Infinio

Improves speech clarity in noisy places and reduces listening effort. It is designed to adjust sound levels automatically and connect seamlessly to smartphones and similar devices.

Key Features:

  • Compatible with CROS for one-sided hearing loss
  • Roger Direct ready for classrooms and busy spaces
  • Connects to 8 devices, streams to 2 at once

Phonak CROS Infinio

Phonak CROS Infinio is built to address single-sided deafness. With Phonak’s smart technology, it can send sound from the weak ear to the stronger one, making you hear sounds efficiently, no matter where it is coming from.

Key Features:

  • Hands-free calling and streaming
  • Rechargeable battery
  • Universal Bluetooth connection

Frequently Asked Questions About Phonak Hearing Aids

What are the levels of Phonak Lumity?

Phonak Lumity comes in four levels: L90, L70, L50, and L30. L90 has the most advanced features, while L30 covers the basics.

What is Phonak Life?

Phonak Audéo Life™ Lumity is the first waterproof rechargeable hearing aid. It is perfect for people who are inclined to sports or water activities, or who live an active lifestyle.

How is Phonak Life different from Phonak Paradise?

Both share advanced features, but Phonak Life offers stronger water resistance, making it better for exercise, swimming, or humid environments.
