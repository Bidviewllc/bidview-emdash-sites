Current Widex Hearing Aid Models

Widex Allure

The Widex Allure carries the brand’s most advanced hearing aid platform, offering the perfect blend of cutting-edge technology and elegant design. It is crafted to deliver the most natural hearing experience possible, helping you reconnect with the world through clear speech, accurate environmental sound processing, and intuitive adjustments.

Key Features:

  • Powered by the W1 Chip: Widex’s fastest chip delivers quick, clear, and natural sound.
  • Allure PureSound™ with ZeroDelay Technology: Helps you hear speech without distortion or echo, creating a more lifelike experience.
  • Speech Enhancer Pro: Lowers background noise so voices stand out, making conversations easier—even in noisy places.
  • Dynamic Feedback Controller: Adapts to your ear’s shape to reduce annoying whistling or squealing sounds.
  • Smooth Streaming: Enjoy music, podcasts, and calls with steady sound—no sudden volume changes or outside interference.
  • Widex Allure App: Easily adjust settings and personalize your hearing experience right from your smartphone.

Widex SmartRIC

The Widex SmartRIC makes speech easier to understand in noisy places with its unique L-shaped design. It offers up to 37 hours of battery life and a portable charger that holds a week’s worth of power. Its advanced microphone cover reduces background noise, so voices come through clearly.

Key Features:

  • Works with the Moment app for easy adjustments
  • Bluetooth streaming for iPhone® and Android™
  • Tinnitus relief with Zen therapy sounds

Widex Moment Sheer RIC

The Widex Moment Sheer RIC has a sleek design and an improved charger. Using dual AI sound processing, it delivers natural listening even in tough environments. Available in both rechargeable and disposable battery options, it lasts up to 29 hours per charge (16 hours with streaming).

Key Features:

  • Bluetooth streaming for iPhone® and Android™
  • Moment app for personalized settings
  • Zen programs for tinnitus relief

Widex Moment RIC

The Widex Moment RIC is small but powerful, processing sound so quickly that speech feels clear and natural. Studies show 91% of users prefer its sound quality over older hearing aids. It works for mild to severe hearing loss and is available with rechargeable or disposable batteries.

Key Features:

  • Bluetooth streaming for iPhones and Androids
  • Customizable sound through the Moment app
  • Tinnitus relief with Zen therapy

Widex Moment BTE

The Widex Moment BTE (behind-the-ear) uses SoundSense Learn, which adapts automatically to your listening preferences. It’s a strong option for mild to severe hearing loss and comes with a rechargeable or size 13 disposable battery.

Key Features:

  • Bluetooth streaming for phones and TVs
  • AI sound learning with SoundSense Learn
  • Zen therapy for tinnitus management

Widex Moment CROS

The Widex Moment CROS is designed for people with hearing in only one ear. It wirelessly sends sound from your non-hearing ear to your better ear, helping with directionality and speech clarity. It’s energy-efficient and can be used as a CROS or BiCROS solution.

Key Features:

  • Advanced CROS technology for single-sided hearing loss
  • Energy-efficient design for longer use
  • Clear sound in noisy environments

Widex Moment ITE

The Widex Moment ITE (in-the-ear) hearing aid is custom-made for your ear, giving you comfort and a secure fit. It provides clear sound naturally and is a great choice for mild to moderate hearing loss. Battery options (size 10 or 312) last up to 150 hours depending on use.

Key Features:

  • Custom fit for comfort and security
  • Bluetooth connectivity with app support
  • Zen programs for tinnitus relief
