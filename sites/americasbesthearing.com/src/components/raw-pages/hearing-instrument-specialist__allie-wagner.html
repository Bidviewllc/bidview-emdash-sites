Allie Wagner, HIS

Hearing Instrument Specialist
Location: Mendota Heights, MN

Allie Wagner is a hearing instrument specialist providing hearing tests, hearing aids, and other hearing care services in Mendota Heights, MN.

Allie Wagner began his career in audiology after responding to an advertisement for the field. While the beginning may not have been a dramatic story, it quickly grew into a genuine passion for helping people through better hearing. Today, Allie is dedicated to empowering his patients by reconnecting them with the people, activities, and communities that enrich their lives.

In his work as a Hearing Instrument Specialist, Allie focuses on more than just hearing aids, he is committed to restoring the confidence and joy that come with improved communication and clearer sound.

Outside the office, Allie’s dedication extends to his family and hobbies. Growing up on a farm in South Dakota instilled in him a lifelong love for the outdoors, which he now enjoys through hunting and golfing with his sons. He also has a passion for woodworking, crafting everything from picture frames to grandfather clocks with patience and creativity.

Family and friends often describe Allie as a little unconventional, known for his loud laughter, self-made jokes, and moments of playful embarrassment in public. He embraces these quirks wholeheartedly, believing they add to the warmth and authenticity he brings to both his personal and professional life.
