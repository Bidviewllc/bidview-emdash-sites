Current ReSound Hearing Aid Models

ReSound Enzo IA

The ReSound Enzo IA is designed for severe to profound hearing loss. It is the world’s smallest rechargeable Super Power hearing aid and the first of its kind to support Bluetooth LE Audio and Auracast broadcast streaming.

With a stylish, durable design, the ReSound Enzo IA is built for the most challenging listening situations. It offers exceptional clarity, feedback control, and all-day battery life.

Key Features:

  • Clear Focus: Automatically reduces background noise while highlighting speech.
  • 360 All-Around listening: Naturally shifts attention between voices and surrounding sounds.
  • Comfortable and weatherproof: Lightweight, all-day wear with protective nanocoating.
  • Flexible connectivity: Supports Bluetooth® LE Audio, Auracast™, and direct streaming from multiple devices.

ReSound Vivia

The ReSound Vivia™ is designed to make hearing feel natural, no matter where you are. It uses advanced technology that works with your brain to reduce effort and make voices clearer in both quiet and noisy settings.

Key Features:

  • All-day rechargeable power, even with streaming
  • Weatherproof protection from water and dust
  • Bluetooth® & Auracast™ connectivity to phones, TVs, and more
  • Auracast Assistant for public announcements in airports, theaters, and more

ReSound Savi

The ReSound Savi™ is lightweight, comfortable, and simple to use. It keeps you connected all day with clear sound and easy streaming to your favorite devices.

Key Features:

  • All-day power, even when streaming
  • Water- and dust-resistant design
  • Bluetooth® & Auracast™ connections for calls, TV, and music
  • Auracast Assistant for public announcements
  • Hands-free calling with a quick tap

ReSound Nexia

The ReSound Nexia provides a natural listening experience with Organic Hearing™ technology. It streams directly to iPhones, iPads, many Android devices, and even works as a headset for Mac computers. For people with one-sided hearing loss, it includes a CROS system for balanced hearing.

Key Features:

  • Latest Bluetooth® for strong sound and long battery life
  • Auracast broadcast audio for public streams
  • Smart 3D app for adjustments and tinnitus management
  • CROS option for single-sided hearing loss

ReSound Omnia RIE & Mini RIE

The ReSound Omnia RIE and Mini RIE offer natural sound in comfortable, discreet designs. They work well for mild to severe hearing loss and come in rechargeable or traditional battery styles.

Key Features:

  • Bluetooth® streaming with iPhone®, iPad®, and Android™
  • “Check My Fit” tool to ensure proper placement
  • Smart 3D app for sound adjustments and tinnitus relief
  • Relaxation tools with the ReSound Relief app

ReSound Omnia ITE

The ReSound Omnia ITE sits inside your ear for a secure, custom fit. It reduces wind noise, making conversations clearer outdoors, and is available in multiple custom styles like ITC, CIC, and IIC.

Key Features:

  • Custom fit for comfort and security
  • Bluetooth® streaming with phones and tablets
  • Smart 3D app for adjustments and tinnitus support
  • Works for mild to profound hearing loss

ReSound Omnia BTE & Power BTE

The ReSound Omnia BTE and Power BTE are designed for people with severe to profound hearing loss. They make speech up to 150% clearer in noisy settings, helping you stay connected in tough listening environments.

Key Features:

  • Bluetooth® streaming and hands-free calls

  • Smart 3D app for control and tinnitus relief

Frequently Asked Questions About ReSound Hearing Aids

What are the levels of ReSound technology?

The newest Omnia hearing aids come in three levels: 9 (premium), 7 (mid-level), and 5 (basic).

How do I adjust my ReSound hearing aids?

You can use the ReSound Smart 3D app, available for iPhone® and Android™. It allows you to adjust sound, manage tinnitus, and even get remote support from your audiologist.

How long do ReSound hearing aids last?

Most last about 5 years, depending on care. Many people upgrade earlier to take advantage of new features and technology.
