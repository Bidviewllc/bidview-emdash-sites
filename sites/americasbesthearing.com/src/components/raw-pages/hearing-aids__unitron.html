Frequently Asked Questions About Unitron Hearing Aids

What is the difference between Unitron Blu and Vivante?

Vivante (V) is the newest generation of Unitron RIC hearing aids, offering improved sound processing and app support. Blu (B) remains the current option for custom in-the-ear devices.

What technology levels are available?

Unitron hearing aids come in four levels: 3, 5, 7, and 9. Higher levels (like 9) offer stronger noise reduction and more automatic adjustments.

How long does it take to charge Unitron hearing aids?

Charging takes about 5–7 hours. A green light indicates when the devices are fully charged.
