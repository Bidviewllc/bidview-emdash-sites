Frequently Asked Questions About Signia Hearing Aids

What is the newest Signia technology?

The AX platform is Signia’s latest. Models like Styletto AX, Pure AX, and Insio Charge&Go AX use Augmented Focus technology to make speech clearer, even in noisy environments.

What technology levels are available for Pure Charge&Go AX?

There are five levels: 1AX, 2AX, 3AX, 5AX, and 7AX. Higher numbers mean more advanced features.

Can I adjust my Signia hearing aids myself?

Yes. With the Signia app, you can adjust volume, switch listening programs, or even change sound tone (bass/treble) from your phone.
